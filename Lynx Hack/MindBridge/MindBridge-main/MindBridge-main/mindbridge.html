Our mission

Built for the gap between okay and crisis.

Most people who need mental health support are not in crisis - they're just stuck. They don't know where to start, what's free, or whether what they're feeling even "counts." MindBridge exists to close that gap.

We connect people in Denver - students at CU Denver, MSU Denver, and CCD, workers, parents, youth, anyone - to real mental health resources at every level. No gatekeeping. No judgment.

What we believe
Community first
Healing happens in connection. Peer voices matter as much as clinical resources.
No cost should be a barrier
Every resource we surface has a free or low-cost option. We refuse to leave anyone out.
Anonymity protects
The community feed is anonymous by design. Sharing is safer when you're not identified.
AI as a bridge, not a replacement
Our navigator helps you find the next step - it never replaces a real human connection.
