You check your bank account and see a charge you don’t recognize. Then it hits you — that free trial from eight months ago that you completely forgot to cancel.

You’re not alone. 61% of people are rethinking their paid subscriptions right now. 26% have already cancelled one, and 24% are still just thinking about it — not acting. Why? Because we forget. Because free trials trap us.

Subscriptions are scattered across multiple email accounts, with billing names we barely recognize. We don’t always know what better alternatives exist. People lose over $1,000 every year because of this.

The most forgotten subscriptions aren’t even obvious: 37% forget their mobile plan, 30% forget their internet, and 22% forget streaming services. This is the subscription paradox. Subscriptions are great, but we lose track. We don’t know where our money is going, and it quietly piles up.

Existing apps like Rocket Money, Truebill, Trim, and Bobby only show you a list. No alternatives, no personalization, no action. That’s not enough.

So we built Subsave. Subsave is an AI-powered subscription watchdog built on Google Gemini. It finds your subscriptions, analyzes them, finds better alternatives, and helps you take action — all in one place. It’s the only tool that does all four.

When you open Subsave, it asks who you are: student, veteran, freelancer, healthcare worker. Our AI engine, Player, uses this to find deals built specifically for your situation. That personalization is what separates us.

Then you hit scan. Player parses your transaction history, detects every recurring charge — even across different billing names and accounts — and scores each one by how much you could save. $130 a month across eight subscriptions — that’s over $1,500 a year just disappearing.

Each card gives you three actions: cancel it, get a cancellation guide, or find something cheaper. This is optimization made simple. Player finds better options and presents them one by one — monthly savings, yearly savings, and a personalized reason why it fits you.

Swipe right to adopt, swipe left to skip. One swipe just replaced LinkedIn Premium with a completely free alternative — $480 saved per year from a single decision.

The dashboard pulls everything together: original spend, optimized spend, monthly and yearly savings, and a 12-month projection of your savings growing over time. Full visibility, nothing hidden.

If you have questions, Player is there as a chat assistant. It responds based on your actual data, not generic advice. And if the AI gets overloaded, our smart fallback system kicks in automatically, so advice never stops.

This is not just about subscriptions. It’s about control. Awareness. Making sure your money works for you.

Subsave runs on React on the front end, Node.js, and a REST API on the backend with a bank API pulling real transaction data. Functional, scalable, built in one week.
